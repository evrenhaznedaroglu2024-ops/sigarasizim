# Privacy Policy for Sigarasizim

Effective date: 2026-04-01

Sigarasizim respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app and how that information is handled.

## 1. Information stored in the app

Sigarasizim stores the following information locally on your device so the app can function correctly:

- your quit date and time
- smoking habit settings such as cigarettes per day, cigarettes per pack, pack price and minutes per cigarette
- notification preference
- selected language
- progress-related counters such as reset count

This information is stored locally on your device using app storage. It is not used to create a personal account inside the app.

## 2. Advertising

Sigarasizim uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob and its partners may collect certain device or usage data to provide, personalize, limit or measure ads, subject to their own policies and applicable law.

This may include:

- device identifiers
- IP address
- app interaction data
- diagnostics and advertising performance data

For more information, please review:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

## 3. Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app may schedule daily reminder notifications on your device. Notification preferences are stored locally and can be turned off at any time from inside the app.

## 4. Health-related information

The app may display informational progress and recovery milestones related to smoking cessation. This content is for general informational purposes only and is not medical advice.

## 5. Data sharing

The app itself does not require account registration and does not knowingly sell your personal information.

However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may process limited data as described above for advertising-related purposes.

## 6. Children

Sigarasizim is not designed specifically for children.

## 7. Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the app simple and limit unnecessary data handling. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 8. Your choices

You can:

- stop using the app at any time
- disable notifications from the app settings
- remove local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage on your device

## 9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on the published policy page.

## 10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can use the support or contact information published with the app listing.
