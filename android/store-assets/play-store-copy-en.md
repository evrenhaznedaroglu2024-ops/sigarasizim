# Google Play Copy

## App Name
Sigarasizim

## Short Description
Track your smoke-free progress, savings and recovery with calm breathing support.

## Full Description
Sigarasizim is a simple and motivating companion for your quit-smoking journey.

It helps you stay focused by showing your progress in a clear, calm and encouraging way.

With Sigarasizim you can:

- track how long you have stayed smoke-free
- see how many cigarettes you have not smoked
- calculate how much money you have saved
- measure how much time you have gained
- follow health recovery milestones after quitting
- use a breathing exercise during craving moments
- receive daily motivation reminders

Instead of a complicated experience, Sigarasizim focuses on clarity, consistency and encouragement. The app is designed to make your daily progress visible so that every smoke-free day feels meaningful.

Main features:

- Smoke-free timer
- Cigarettes avoided and money saved
- Health milestone timeline
- Progress and achievement screens
- Built-in breathing exercise
- Turkish and English language support
- Free to use with ads
